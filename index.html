Project

LiveRiot provides musicians and their fans an easy way to document live events and to curate these events/videos in an online community dedicated to their local music.

LiveRiot gives users the technology to share music like never before. With our audio syncing technology and powerful social media tools, we make it easy to share, browse, and discover live music.
